The marathon's origin is connected to the Battle of Marathon in 490 BC during the Greco-Persian Wars. After the Athenians defeated the Persians, a messenger named Pheidippides ran 42 kilometers from Marathon to Athens to deliver the news. Upon reaching Athens, he collapsed and diedğŸª¦ (maybe he did not do proper training before). This run inspired the modern marathon, first held in the modern Olympics in 1896.
